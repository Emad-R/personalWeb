A New Chapter

Bangladesh to USA

One of the biggest dreams of many, many Bangladesh residents is to go to a prosperous nation (USA, especially), and start a life that's filled with prosperous promises. My life back in Bangladesh was filled with financial burdens and difficulties, and fortunately my family and I have been granted the opportunity to immigrate to the United States. Adapting to such a change in enviornment was certainly not easy, however with the help of our relatives, we managed to settle in.

Life in the US

One flawed thinking that Bangladesh residents and I used to believe is that America is simply a place where money just happens to conveniently grow on trees for you. After living in the US for nearly 10 years, I have certainly changed my perception of America. Without hard work and dedication, a successful life will cease to exist. I changed who I used to be, I now grasped the concept of dedication, and I have put relentless effort to do the best that I can. Numerous successful people, have histories of struggles and failures. Frankly, if there's one thing that I learned in these 10 years, it's that failure exists not to bring one down, but to teach one to get up.
